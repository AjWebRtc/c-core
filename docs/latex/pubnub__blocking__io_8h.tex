\subsection{Detailed Description}
This is the \char`\"{}\-Blocking I/\-O\char`\"{} A\-P\-I of the Pubnub client library. Functions here influence the way that Pubnub client library works with lower levels (the T\-C\-P/\-I\-P stack).

It is available on platforms that support setting blocking / non-\/blocking behavior. Be aware that, even on such platforms, most of these functions provide more of a hint, rather than a command.
